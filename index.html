What are we doing here?

This website is a school assignment, meant to show up on the first Google search page when someone looks for "weird unsettling films". It's a genre that's hard to describe exactly, because they are all slightly different. Some are more thought-provoking and artistic, some are just an uncanny valley-level of badly done.

Whatever your interests, there is likely to be a weird, unsettling, and generally strange film that might appeal to you. This site will be updated regularly, so please keep checking back! You might stumble upon your next favourite movie!
