Weird Unsettling Films -- about

What is this website even ABOUT?

Have you ever seen a movie that puzzles you? Maybe it’s a horror movie, but it unsettled you in some way? Maybe it’s just a very difficult movie to explain?

This website is an ever-updating list of films that fit this category for me, and accounts for suggestions from those who have viewed the site and been kind enough to submit a recommendation. I do my best to keep this website up to date, but it is a student project as well as a passion project.

The goal of this website as a student project is to be on the first page of google when someone searches “weird unsettling films”; a tall order, because it seems that few people seek out this type of media. I rely on word-of-mouth, and various SEO experiments to help this website reach a larger audience.

If you feel that you run a different website, with similar interests and recommendations, please do link to my website! I will happily link to your website as well, if it makes sense to do so. Below, I will attach a few promotional banners that you may use at your discretion.

As always, recommendations for future films are always welcome. Please go to the “Contact” page to submit a request, and I will happily watch the movie and create a short review. It may take some time for me to update this website accordingly, but I value all the feedback I receive.

I hope you enjoy this website, and find a strange, unsettling, film that you like!
